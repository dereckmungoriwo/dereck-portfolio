About Me

Blending Culinary Mastery with Data-Driven Innovation

The Journey

From Kitchen Creativity to Data Precision

I've spent the last six years mastering the culinary arts, but I've always been drawn to understanding the "why" behind the kitchen. This curiosity led me to ask three obsessive questions:

  • Why do certain dishes succeed while others fail?
  • How can operations run smarter, not just harder?
  • Where can technology elevate the food industry beyond tradition?

My culinary foundation began in 2018, where I developed precision, creativity, and the ability to perform under pressure. But as I watched technology reshape every industry, I knew I needed to evolve beyond traditional culinary skills.

The Johns Hopkins University

Data Science Specialization

Rutgers University

Supply Management Program

Today, I sit at a unique intersection—combining hands-on kitchen expertise with Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI to solve real operational challenges. I build dashboards that track food costs in real time, analyze sales patterns to refine menu offerings, optimize vendor relationships, and identify bottlenecks before they affect service.

I don't just follow recipes — I find patterns. I don't just manage inventory — I forecast demand.

The food industry is being revolutionized by technology, and I'm built for this moment. I am actively pursuing opportunities in culinary operations, food tech, and hospitality analytics, where I can help businesses make informed, data-driven decisions while maintaining an unwavering focus on what matters most: exceptional food and unforgettable experiences.
